How pages that generate random images can help you in your projects

Probably when you refresh this page, the images showed here will be completely different. This is because to build this site, I used a platform to random generate them. It's a practical way if you just want a standard way to present images in your website.

For the images of this post I used a platform called Unsplash. Unsplash provides freely-usable images helping developers to have high-quality pictures on their website. There are some similar platforms like, another example is Picsum, that uses a simple and intuitive interface to help their users.
